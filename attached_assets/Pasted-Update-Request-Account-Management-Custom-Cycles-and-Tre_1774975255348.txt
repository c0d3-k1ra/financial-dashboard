How the "25th Reset" Logic Works
In your code, the Agent will now need to use a "Current Cycle" function instead of a standard calendar month.

Before the 25th: The app shows data starting from the 25th of the previous month.

On the 25th: The "Current Spend" resets to zero, and the "Starting Balance" is updated with the cumulative total of your accounts at that moment.

How "Transfers" work in your App:
Since you asked for transfers, the logic looks like this:

Bank A → Bank B: Account A (-100), Account B (+100). Impact on Budget: 0.

Bank A → Credit Card: Account A (-100), CC Outstanding (-100). Impact on Budget: This is a "Debt Payment," not a new expense.
